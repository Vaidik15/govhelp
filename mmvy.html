Mukhyamantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana (MMVY)

What Is MMVY Scholarship MP 2023?

The Mukhya Mantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana is a prestigious scholarship scheme that is only available for permanent residents of Madhya Pradesh state. To avail of the benefit of this scheme, you must have passed the Class 12th examination with a minimum of 70% marks if you studied on the state board and around 85% marks for CBSE and ICSE board. The annual income of your family must be below Rupees 6,00,000. The applicants will be eligible for many benefits under this scholarship scheme. You can fill up the application form from the official website of the organization.


Mantri Medhavi Vidyarthi Yojana :- https://scholarshipportal.mp.nic.in/


Benefits Of MMVY Scholarship -

The following benefits will be provided to the candidates in this scholarship scheme:-

  • The students enrolling in an engineering program at a government/non-government college will receive full fees for the course. However, the students enrolling in a private college will receive INR 1.5 Lakh or the actual course fee (whichever is less).
  • The students enrolling in a medical degree program at a government/private medical/dental college will also receive the full tuition fee.
  • The students enrolling in a law degree program at National Law Universities in India will also receive full course fees under this scheme.
  • Besides this, the students pursuing an undergraduate or integrated postgraduate program from a recognized institution will also receive full tuition fees


MMVY Scholarship Eligibility Criteria -

The applicant must follow the following eligibility criteria to be eligible for this scholarship scheme:-

  • The applicant must be a resident of Madhya Pradesh.
  • He/ She must have passed Class 12 with 70% or more marks (if appeared through the Secondary Education Board of Madhya Pradesh) or have obtained 85% or more marks (if appeared through CBSE/ICSE Board).
  • The applicant must have an annual parent/guardian income of less than INR 6 lakh from all sources.
  • The applicant must have enrolled in an undergraduate-level of course at a recognized university or institution.
  • Engineering Aspirants must have qualified JEE Main exam with a rank less than 1,50,000.
  • Medical Aspirants must have obtained admission to a private/ government medical/ dental college through NEET merit.
  • Law Aspirants must have cleared the CLAT exam and obtained admission to any National Law University or Delhi University.


Documents Required -

The following documents must be submitted by the candidates while filling up the application form :-

  • Aadhaar Card of the Candidate
  • Annual Income Certificate
  • Domicile of Madhya Pradesh
  • Class 12th Marksheet
  • Proof of Admission
  • 10th Scorecard and Certificates
  • Photo of Applicant
  • Complete Bank Details like Name of Bank, IFSC or MICR Code, Branch Name, etc
  • Contact Number


Terms and Conditions :-

The applicant will have to follow the following terms and conditions to apply for this scholarship scheme:-

  • Applicants who are taking the benefits of this scheme have to complete their higher education in the given time period.
  • Charges payable to students of government institutions will be payable in the account of the institution, while fees payable to students studying in private institutions will be payable in the account of students.
  • In case of the student receives support from any other plan of the State or Central Government, he/ she will get the odd amount.
  • All the recipients of this scheme and their families can return the amount given under this scheme to other beneficiaries like them.


MMVY Scholarship Application Procedure :-

Apply for the scholarship you will have to follow the simple procedure given below :-

  • First, click on the link given here to go to the MMVY web page for the scholarship official website.
  • The homepage of the scholarship will open on your screen.
  • Now you have to click on the option called Application present on the menu bar.
  • A drop down menu will open on your screen
  • You have to click on the option called Resister on Portal(New student)
  • A new page will open on your screen
  • You have to fill up the registration form.
  • Include all of your details regarding your education and your personal information.
  • Enter your address
  • Checkmark the declaration
  • Click on the option called Check Form Validation.
  • The application will be successfully submitted.
