AYUSHMAAN BHARAT SCHEME

AYUSHMAN BHARAT DIGITAL MISSION

Ayushman Bharat Yojana or National Health Protection Scheme is a program which aims to provide a service to create a healthy, capable and content new India. Presently it is running as a pilot project in Uttar Pradesh from 4 September 2018. It has two goals, one, creating a network of health and wellness infrastructure across the nation to deliver comprehensive primary healthcare services, and another is to provide insurance cover to at least 40 per cent of India's population which is majorly deprived of secondary and tertiary care services. This centrally sponsored flagship scheme aims to provide an annual health cover of up to Rs. 5 lakh to vulnerable 10 crore families (approximately 50 crore persons - 40% of country's population) based on Socio Economic and Caste Census database.

Registration and Eligibility for Rural Areas :-

The eligibility criteria can be checked on the Ayushman Bharat official website and can be verified as given below:
  • Families living in only one room with kachcharoof and kaccha walls
  • SC/ST households
  • Tribal groups
  • Destitute and manual scavenger families
  • Laborers
  • Families with no adults between 16 and 59 years.
  • Female-headed families with no adult member in the age group of 16-59 years.
  • Families with one disabled member and no adult member with abled-bodies
  • Landless households deriving income from manual casual labours


How to Check your Eligibility Offline?

To register and check your eligibility offline to enroll under the scheme, you can dial the toll-free helpline number 14555. The rest of the process is given below:

  • Visit the siteand enter your mobile number.
  • Enter the Captcha letters displayed on the screen, and click on "Generate OTP".
  • An OTP will be sent to you over a text message on your registered mobile number. Enter this and click on Verify OTP.
  • You can enter your mobile number/ration card number, which directs you to a screen where you can check your eligibility, RSBY URN, and Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) name.
  • If you have your name on the list, it will reflect on the right side of the page.
  • Go to the 'Family Members' tab to view the details of the beneficiary and family member's information covered under the scheme.

Ayushman Card Eligibility Criteria -

  • The applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • The applicant must provide evidence of income that is less than the BPL.
  • There cannot be a pucca residence registered in either of the applicant's names for the applicant or any member of the applicant's family to be eligible for this health benefit.
  • The applicant will not be eligible for this health card under any circumstances if he has previously received assistance from any other type of government housing program.
  • Additionally, negotiations are underway to expand the scope of this capability in order to assist the greatest number of people with the Ayushman Bharat Card.


Documents Required to Ayushman Card Apply Online -

For the PMJAY Portal at PMJAY.gov.in to generate an ABHA Card Number, the basic documents listed below must be submitted. Therefore, please gather all of these documents before you begin the Ayushman Card Apply Online Registration process.

  • Aadhar Card linked with Mobile Number.
  • Bank Account Number.
  • Medical Certificate.
  • Declaration regarding Medical Condition.
  • Income Proof.
  • Domicile Certificate.
  • Caste Certificate


Benefits of Ayushman Bharat Yojana -

Several Ayushman Bharat Yojana 2023 benefits can be claimed once your ABHA registration is complete.

  • Using your ABHA Card Number, you can be admitted to any hospital that takes part in this program.
  • All illnesses, minor and big, are completely free to be treated with this Ayushman Bharat Card.
  • You are entitled to free medical care at any hospital for up to Rs 5 lakhs per year for a single member of your household.
  • The Ayushman Bharat Yojana 2023 will pay for all diagnostic procedures, lab work, medications, and admission costs.


How to Apply Online for Ayushman Bharat Card?

  • Candidates must access pmjay.gov.in and click the Am I Eligible option.
  • Enter the OTP you received on your registered mobile number along with your Aadhar number.
  • If you are qualified to use this link, the Online Application Link will appear on the following page.
  • Enter your name, address, and other necessary information under Personal and Family Details.
  • When you submit the form, your screen will display the ABHA Card Number.
  • Your family members applications will also be approved in a few days, and this is your Ayushman Bharat Card number.
  • Your Dashboard will then display the Ayushman Bharat Card 2023 that was created.
